Congregations, Districts & Presbyteries

Define every unit in the hierarchy. Each entry maps to a Firebase data path. Users are scoped to their unit and can only see data within it.

📐 PCG Data Hierarchy
congregations/{id}/ — Congregation data (written by the main ChurchOS app)
districts/{id}/congregations/{cid}/summary — District rollup (published automatically)
presbyteries/{id}/districts/{did}/ — Presbytery rollup
national/presbyteries/{pid}/ — National rollup
District, Presbytery, and National entries here are used for scoping hierarchy admin accounts and are registered in up_scopes/.
